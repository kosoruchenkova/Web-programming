About our restaurant

Welcome to Sakura

Established in 2025, our restaurant combines the traditions of Japanese cuisine with a modern approach to cooking. Our chefs have been trained in Japan and have many years of experience working with seafood.

We pay special attention to the quality of ingredients: fish and seafood are delivered daily, rice for sushi is grown on special plantations, and we get vegetables from local farmers.

Our Chef

Satoshi Yamamoto

Our chef Satoshi Yamamoto is a true master of Japanese cuisine with over 20 years of experience. Born and raised in Tokyo, where he started his career at the famous Sushi restaurant.

Satoshi trained under the best sushi masters in Japan and then perfected his art at Michelin restaurants in Osaka and Kyoto. His philosophy: "Sushi is not just food, it is meditation embodied in rice and fish".

In 2019, Satoshi received the Golden Knife Award at the Tokyo Chef Competition for innovation in traditional cuisine.

Every morning, Satoshi personally selects the fish from the supplier and then goes out to the guests to hear their impressions. He says, "The guest's happiness is my main reward."
